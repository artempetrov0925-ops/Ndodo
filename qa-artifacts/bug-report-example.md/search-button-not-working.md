**Title:** Search button does not respond after entering text in the search field

**Environment:**
- Browser: Chrome 121
- OS: Windows 11
- Device: Desktop

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Open the homepage
2. Enter any text in the search field
3. Click the "Search" button

**Expected Result:**
Search results should appear.

**Actual Result:**
Nothing happens after clicking the "Search" button.

**Priority:** High
